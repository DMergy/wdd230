Site Purpose

As a chamber it is our goal to help our local businesses and organizations be successful. We strive to offer events and activities that allow our business owners to network, talk about their business and build long lasting relationships within the community. We offer opportunities for professional development and personal growth through monthly events including our Breakfast before Business and our Business After Hours as well as with our lunch and learn events. If you are interested in hosting, be sure to check out our sponsorships page!
