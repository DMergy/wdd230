Voted Business of the Year: Maggie's on the Prom
Our team of culinary experts source, prepare and create the very best, locally sourced food. We are passionate about the ingredients we bring into the restaurant to put on your plate, and we only choose the freshest products from the closest purveyors we can source. We refuse to compromise on quality in our restaurant. We source our ingredients from more than 20 fisheries, farms, foragers, bakeries, breweries and wineries located within Oregon and Washington. Our goal is to highlight the bounty available on the Oregon Coast.
